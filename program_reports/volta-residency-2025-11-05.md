## Executive Summary

Volta is Atlantic Canada's leading innovation hub and AI-focused startup accelerator, operating from downtown Halifax since 2013. The organization offers a structured three-stage residency program (Signal ‚Üí Catalyst ‚Üí Growth) designed to help founders validate ideas, build MVPs, and scale to $1M+ Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR).

Unlike traditional government funding programs, Volta provides a comprehensive support ecosystem combining coaching, mentorship, workspace access, and connections to funding sources. The first two stages (Signal and Catalyst) are completely free, while the Growth stage requires $1,200/year in administrative fees to cover coaching and mentor matching.

Volta also operates specialized programs including an AI-First Residency Program (partnership with Dalhousie University launched August 2024), the Scale AI Accelerate program (providing up to $14,000 for supply chain AI companies), the LEAP pre-accelerator program (for companies preparing for top-tier accelerators), and a Cleantech partnership with Halifax Partnership.

The program is designed for early-stage technology startups with at least one founder based in Atlantic Canada, particularly those focused on AI, high-growth tech sectors, and scalable solutions. Since inception, Volta alumni have raised over $374 million and created 3,200+ jobs, demonstrating significant ecosystem impact.

**Quick Facts:**
- **Administered by:** Volta (Private Non-Profit Corporation)
- **Geographic Scope:** Atlantic Canada (NS, NB, PE, NL) - at least one founder must be based in region
- **Program Type:** Startup Accelerator/Incubator with specialized cohort programs
- **Amount Range:** $0 (main residency) to $14,000 (Scale AI Accelerate program)
- **Coverage:** Coaching, mentorship, workspace access, investor connections, funding navigation, specialized programming
- **Repayable:** No (non-dilutive for grants; administrative fees for Growth stage)
- **Application:** Continuous intake - rolling applications year-round

## Program Structure

Volta operates multiple programs serving different startup stages and sectors. The core offering is the three-stage Residency Program, supplemented by specialized programs providing direct funding and targeted support.

### Core Residency Program (3 Stages)

#### Stage 1: Signal (Idea Stage) - FREE

**Purpose:** Work with founders in the idea stage to help them find a Signal in the market and learn the skills needed to test assumptions.

**Target:** Founders with startup ideas who need to validate market fit

**Structure:**
- Virtual delivery
- Regular check-ins with growth coaches (4-6 week cadence)
- Performance evaluated every 90 days

**Cost:** FREE - no fees

**Key Activities:**
- Idea validation
- Problem hypothesis development
- Early market testing
- Learning foundational startup skills

**Exit Criteria:** Ready to move to Catalyst when idea is validated and founder is ready to build MVP

---

#### Stage 2: Catalyst (MVP Stage) - FREE

**Purpose:** Help founders transform validated ideas into functional MVPs and navigate the non-dilutive funding landscape.

**Target:** Startups that have moved beyond the idea stage with early customer validation

**Core Focus:**
- **Crafting MVPs that ignite market success**
- Navigating non-dilutive funding opportunities
- Building product-market fit

**Structure:**
- Virtual delivery
- Regular check-ins with growth coaches (4-6 week cadence)
- KPI establishment using the Value Engine framework
- Performance evaluated every 90 days

**Cost:** FREE - no fees

**Key Activities:**

**1. Validation & Research Phase:**
- Problem hypothesis documentation
- Market research and competitive analysis
- Early adopter funnel development
- Customer discovery interviews
- Interview result analysis

**2. MVP Design Phase:**
- Customer problem mapping
- Feature development and UX design
- Low and high-fidelity wireframing
- Continuous customer discovery testing

**3. MVP Development Phase:**
- Gap analysis for funding, talent, and resources
- Feature-to-requirement conversion
- Iterative product refinement based on feedback

**Funding Navigation Support:**
Volta assists Catalyst residents in accessing non-dilutive funding by:
- Introducing grant opportunities and application strategies
- Connecting founders with partner organizations and advisors
- Helping create funding roadmaps

**Exit Criteria:** Ready to move to Growth when MVP is built and company has customers/users ready to iterate

---

#### Stage 3: Growth (Scaling Stage) - $1,200/year

**Purpose:** Support companies with products and customers as they iterate and scale to $1M ARR.

**Target:** Startups with functional products, active customers/users, and revenue potential

**Structure:**
- Virtual delivery (default)
- Optional space rental add-on available in downtown Halifax facility
- Continued coaching and mentor matching
- Access to subject-matter experts
- Performance evaluated every 90 days

**Cost:** $1,200 per year (administrative fee to cover Volta's overhead costs for coach/mentor matching)

**Key Activities:**
- Product iteration based on customer feedback
- Revenue growth strategies
- Scaling operations
- Fundraising preparation and connections
- Market expansion planning

**Support Provided:**
- Matching with specialized coaches and advisors
- Connection to investor network
- Access to subject-matter experts
- Community of scaling founders

**Launched:** August 2024

**Delivery:** Rolling enrollment (continuous intake)

- Uses Volta's standard three-stage residency model

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Clear target customer and problem to solve
- At least one co-founder based in Atlantic Canada
- Technical co-founder capable of building AI solutions
- AI integral to the product and its core innovation
- Solution designed to scale in user base, data processing, and functionality
- Commitment to building AI ethically, ensuring fairness, transparency, and regulatory compliance
- Each team member must dedicate at least 20 hours per week to the startup
- Must incorporate AI into core business models, products, and operations

**Support Provided:**
- **From Volta:** Mentorship, operational resources, coaching through residency framework
- **From Dalhousie:** Specialized workshops, technical training, talent pipeline access
- Reduced administrative burden on founders seeking resources

**Cost:** Follows standard residency pricing (Signal and Catalyst free, Growth $1,200/year)

## Strategic Analysis

### Program Intent and Ecosystem Role

**Why Volta Exists:**

Volta was founded to address critical gaps in Atlantic Canada's startup ecosystem:
1. **Lack of structured support:** Few resources for early-stage tech founders in region
2. **Talent retention:** Need to keep tech talent in Atlantic Canada rather than losing to Toronto/Boston/Silicon Valley
3. **Market access:** Bridge between regional founders and global markets/capital
4. **Knowledge economy development:** Create high-value tech jobs in traditionally resource-based economies

**Strategic Context:**
