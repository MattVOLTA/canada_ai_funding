Program Overview

The BDC Data to AI Program addresses the AI adoption gap for Canadian SMBs. Launched December 4, 2024, this program provides a unique "advisory + financing" bundle that helps established businesses identify, plan, and implement AI solutions without requiring upfront capital.

Why This Program Exists:

  • Only 8% of Canadian SMBs have adopted AI (vs. 29% in the U.S.)
  • SMBs lack internal expertise to evaluate vendors and integrate AI
  • AI adoption requires upfront capital many SMBs can't self-finance
  • Canada Digital Adoption Program (CDAP) ended March 31, 2025, creating a funding gap

What You Get:

  • AI Readiness Assessment: Current state analysis and capability gaps identification
  • Opportunity Identification: High-impact AI use cases prioritized by ROI
  • Technology Selection: Vendor evaluation and recommendations
  • Implementation Planning: Detailed roadmap with milestones and risk mitigation
  • Financial Planning: Cost breakdown and ROI projections
  • Term Loan Financing: 100% project cost coverage with repayment up to 8 years

Who Should Apply

This program is designed for established SMBs that recognize AI is critical for competitiveness but lack expertise and capital to implement independently.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Canadian business with 2+ years operating revenue
  • Good business and personal credit history
  • Genuine AI adoption need (not pure R&D development)
  • Positive cash flow or clear path to profitability
  • Ability to service loan payments from operations

Typical Profile:

Established manufacturer or service business (15-200 employees, $2M-$50M revenue) that knows AI can help but doesn't know where to start or which vendors to use.

Project Types:

  • Customer service automation and chatbots
  • Predictive maintenance for equipment
  • Supply chain optimization
  • Data analytics and business intelligence
  • Cybersecurity enhancements

Application Process

Stage 1: Initial Contact & Discovery (2-4 weeks)

  1. Contact BDC at 1-877-232-2269
  2. Discuss AI adoption needs and goals
  3. Preliminary credit evaluation
  4. Decision to proceed to roadmap development

Stage 2: AI Roadmap Development (4-8 weeks)

  1. Work with assigned BDC advisor to assess current state
  2. Identify high-impact AI opportunities
  3. Develop comprehensive implementation roadmap
  4. Create detailed budget and ROI projections
  5. Receive final roadmap document

Stage 3: Financing Approval (4-7 weeks)

  1. Submit formal loan application with roadmap
  2. Provide financial statements and documentation
  3. Full credit assessment and analysis
  4. Loan approval and documentation
  5. Funding disbursed (typically tied to project milestones)

Total timeline: 2-4 months from first contact to funded

Contact Information

What to Prepare Before Calling:

  • 2-3 years of financial statements
  • Description of AI adoption goals
  • Current technology infrastructure overview
  • Rough budget range

Key Differences from Grants

  • Financing vs. Grant: This is a loan (repayable) not a grant, but with preferential interest rates and flexible terms
  • Advisory Included: Roadmap development is bundled, unlike pure R&D programs
  • 100% Coverage: Can finance full project cost, unlike programs with 50-75% matching
  • 8-Year Repayment: Longer than commercial banks (eases cash flow)
  • Non-Government Assistance: Loan doesn't count toward government assistance caps - can stack with grants
