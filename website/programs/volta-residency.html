Program Overview

Volta is a unique accelerator that provides structured mentorship, coaching, and workspace to early-stage tech founders in Atlantic Canada. Rather than just providing funding, Volta offers a comprehensive ecosystem combining:

Three-Stage Residency Model:

  • Signal Stage (Idea): Validate that your problem is significant and worth solving. Work with growth coaches to test market assumptions.
  • Catalyst Stage (MVP): Design and develop your minimum viable product. Get customer discovery guidance, UX support, and fundraising navigation.
  • Growth Stage (Scaling): Prepare for investment and scale toward $1M+ ARR. Access intensive coaching, investor introductions, and optional co-working space.

What Volta Provides:

  • Growth coaching aligned to your stage
  • Access to domain-specific advisors and mentors
  • Community of fellow founders (peer learning)
  • Structured methodology (Value Engine framework)
  • Investor introductions and network access
  • Connections to government funding programs (IRAP, ACOA, etc.)
  • Pitch support and financial modeling assistance

Eligibility & Fit Assessment

Core Requirements:

  • At least one founder based in Atlantic Canada
  • Full-time commitment to the venture
  • Technology-enabled, high-growth potential solution
  • Global market opportunity (not just local/regional)
  • Coachable, evidence-based mindset

Growth Stage (Additional Requirements):

  • Founders must prove residency in Atlantic Canada
  • Have functional product with customers/users
  • Actively iterating based on market feedback
  • Commit to building material impact in Atlantic Canada

Best For:

Early-stage AI/SaaS companies with passionate founders, validated problems, and commitment to residency in Atlantic Canada. Strong fit for teams building for global markets but rooted in the region.

Not Suitable For:

  • No Atlantic Canada connection or founders
  • Non-tech or pure service businesses without product
  • Part-time side projects
  • Companies planning to relocate outside Atlantic Canada
  • Founders unwilling to follow evidence-based methodology

Specialized Funding Programs

Volta AI Cohort (Scale AI Partnership)

  • Amount: Up to $14,000 per company
  • Focus: Supply chain optimization AI solutions
  • Status: Periodic cohorts (check website for next intake)
  • Includes: Co-working space, mentorship, expert advisors, investor network

Volta Cohort (Investment Program)

  • Amount: $25,000 seed investment
  • Type: Equity-based (investment, not grant)
  • Status: Periodic cohorts
  • Includes: Mentorship, residency, training, credibility with investors

How to Apply

  1. Self-Assess: Verify you meet eligibility criteria and understand your stage (Signal/Catalyst/Growth)
  2. Prepare Your Story: Articulate your problem, evidence of validation, team background, and commitment
  3. Submit Application: Visit https://www.voltaeffect.com/apply-for-residency and complete online form
  4. Volta Conversation: Selected applicants have phone/video call with Volta team (3-7 days)
  5. Decision: Receive acceptance/rejection (typically 2-4 weeks from submission)
  6. Onboarding: Meet growth coach, establish KPIs, join community
