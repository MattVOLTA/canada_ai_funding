Program Overview

The Regional Artificial Intelligence Initiative (RAII) provides funding to help AI startups scale their solutions to market and enable SMEs to integrate AI technologies for productivity and competitiveness gains.

Two Program Streams:

  • Pillar 1 - AI Productization & Commercialization: Supports AI startups and scale-ups to bring innovations from lab to market
  • Pillar 2 - AI Adoption: Helps established SMEs adopt and integrate AI solutions to enhance productivity

Eligibility Highlights

  • Must be incorporated in Canada (not sole proprietor)
  • Minimum 2 years in operation (for for-profit businesses)
  • Must have staffed operating facilities in the RDA region
  • Project cost must be $500,000 - $10,000,000
  • 50% non-government funding MUST be confirmed before applying
  • Technology Readiness Level 7+ (for commercialization stream)

Application Process

  1. Expression of Interest (EOI): Submit abbreviated application (30-day decision)
  2. Full Application: If EOI approved, submit comprehensive business plan (~90 business days to decision)
  3. Total Timeline: 5-7 months from EOI to funding decision
