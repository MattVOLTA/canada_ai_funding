Program Overview

Invest Nova Scotia Accelerate is a milestone-based acceleration program designed to help early-stage Nova Scotia startups become investment-ready. Selected companies receive up to $40,000 in non-dilutive funding (reimbursement-based) plus intensive mentorship, investor introductions, and access to a $100,000+ software perks program.

Program Structure:

  • Application Window: ~4 weeks in summer (June-July)
  • Shortlist Pitches: August for selected applicants
  • 5-Month Intensive: October - February/March
  • Showcase & Announcements: March in following year

What You Get:

  • Up to $40,000 in milestone-based funding (reimbursement model)
  • Weekly one-on-one coaching sessions
  • Monthly investor readiness workshops
  • Investor introductions and pitch support
  • Financial modeling and go-to-market strategy guidance
  • $100K+ in software/SaaS discounts (HubSpot, AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)
  • Access to Invest NS portfolio and follow-on funding opportunities

Eligibility Criteria

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Nova Scotia Registration: Must be NS-registered business in good standing
  • Revenue Threshold: <$1,000,000 in cumulative sales to date
  • Funding Threshold: <$250,000 in equity/convertible debt raised to date
  • Ownership: Founders must be majority owners (51%+)
  • Commitment: Must plan to work full-time with business
  • Stage: Must have proof of concept OR MVP (working prototype)
  • Traction: Evidence of customer validation required
  • Sector: ONLY 5 eligible sectors (see below)
  • Location: Majority of team and leadership in Nova Scotia

The Five Priority Sectors:

  • Agriculture Tech: Addressing global food production challenges
  • Clean Tech: Climate change and environmental solutions
  • Health Tech: New therapeutics, medical devices, digital health
  • Ocean Tech: Ocean health, fisheries, aquaculture, offshore energy
  • Software: AI, computer vision, blockchain, data network effects

Ideal Candidate Profile:

Nova Scotia-based founder(s) with 1-3 years of work on their startup, validated MVP with early customers, and ready to raise $500K-$2M in seed capital within 12-24 months. Founders working full-time with complementary skills (technical + business).
